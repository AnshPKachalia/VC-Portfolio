Research · India Health & Wellness

Ecosystem map

A map of the Indian health and wellness startup landscape across five categories. Hover over any company to see why it is on the map.

Active living & movement
Gyms & studios
Cult.fit
India's dominant omnichannel gym and fitness platform. Unicorn.
Fitpass
Single membership for 8,100+ gyms across India. Asset-light aggregator model.
WTF Gyms
Takes over underperforming local gyms and standardises operations.Tier 2 and 3 focused.
Fitness apps & coaching
HealthifyMe
India's largest AI-powered nutrition and fitness app. 40M users, $145M raised, expanding to the US.
Fittr
Community-first coaching platform started as a WhatsApp group. $19.9M raised. Outcome-driven model.
Fitness challenges & events
Stepsetgo
Gamified walking app that rewards steps with redeemable coins. Makes movement accessible and fun.
Spectacom Global
Builds Indian fitness IPs including Devils Circuit and the Yoddha Race. Obstacle races and mass participation events.
Yoska
Endurance coaching platform that brought Ironman and Hyrox to India. Premium event operator in the triathlon and functional fitness space.
Home & connected fitness
Flexnest
Smart connected cardio equipment — bikes, rowers, treadmills — with integrated app and trainer-led classes. Bootstrapped to ₹22 crore revenue.
Portl
AI smart mirror and compact home gym system. $4M raised from Kalaari Capital and Bharat Innovation Fund.
Aroleap
Wall-mounted digital weights home gym. India's only Tonal equivalent. IIT Delhi founders, patented tech, Shark Tank funded.
Nutrition & supplements
Clean & plant protein
Wellbeing Nutrition
Clean Label Purity Award recipient. Science-led, clean ingredient protein for everyday wellness.
Cosmix
Founded by a PCOS patient turned herbalist. Plant-based, adaptogen-led nutrition built on lived experience. Acquired by Marico.
Only What's Needed
Radical clean label positioning — exactly what the name says. No fillers, no additives, nothing unnecessary.
The Func Lab
Functional nutrition built around specific health goals. Science-first formulations for performance and recovery.
Performance supplements
MuscleBlaze
India's largest sports nutrition brand by volume. Affordable, widely distributed, trusted by gym-goers across all tiers.
AS-IT-IS Nutrition
No frills, no flavour, just raw ingredients. Built a loyal community around transparency and value.
OZiva
Clean, plant-based supplements targeting women and wellness seekers. Strong D2C brand with holistic health positioning.
Alt protein formats
Prot
Ready-to-eat protein cubes. Plant based protein chunk to cook with. Making protein consumption more convenient and accessible beyond powders.
Phyx 44
Protein-forward snack formats. Targeting the gap between supplements and everyday food.
Bombay Hemp Co.
Hemp-based nutrition and wellness products. Early mover in a regulatory grey area with strong brand identity.
Healthy food & meals
Macro-tracked meal delivery
The Kenko Life
Macro-counted, chef-cooked meals on subscription. Expanding beyond Bengaluru. B2C and B2B.
Parafit
Personalised nutrition and fitness coaching with meal tracking. Goal-oriented plans built around individual health data.
6meal
Structured daily meals for fitness-focused consumers. Removes the planning burden of eating for performance.
Health-first QSR & cafes
EatFit
Internet-first healthy food brand under Curefoods. Largest revenue driver in the Curefoods portfolio.
Salad Days
Premium healthy quick-service restaurants. Strong brand in the urban health-conscious dining segment.
Clean snacking
Farmley
Dry fruits and nut-based snacks with clean ingredients. Strong clean snacking report tracking consumer label behaviour.
Open Secret
Healthy versions of favourite junk food — chips, cookies, puffs. Making clean eating feel like a treat not a sacrifice.
Gobble Right
Wholesome, clean ingredient snacks built for everyday health-conscious consumption.
True Elements
Millet-based and whole grain snacks and breakfast. Riding the millet wave with strong clean label credentials.
The Whole Truth
Pioneered radical ingredient transparency in India. Every ingredient visible on front of pack. Strong cult following.
Yoga Bar
Protein bars, oats and granola with clean positioning. Acquired by ITC, validating the category at scale.
Slurrp Farm
Millet-based foods for children and families. Strong at making healthy eating a family habit from early.
Health tracking
Wearables & fitness trackers
Ultrahuman
Premium metabolic health wearable. CGM integration, ring, and app. $55M raised. Building the science-first tracker for serious health optimisers. Global.
Gabit
Affordable smart ring for health tracking. India-first approach to wearable health data at accessible price points.
Bettr
Health wearable focused on workout specific and readiness metrics. Targeting the performance-conscious Indian consumer.
Noise
Mass market smartwatch brand. Large distribution footprint making health tracking accessible to the broader middle market.
Nutrition tracking
HealthifyMe
India's most comprehensive food and nutrition logger. Indian food database of 10,000+ items makes it uniquely relevant locally.
Fittr
Combines nutrition tracking with community accountability and coach-led guidance for measurable outcomes.
Non-invasive biomarker tracking
Tracky
Non-invasive health monitoring using emerging sensor technology. Early stage but targeting the frontier of at-home diagnostics.
Temple
Continuous, non-invasive health insights without blood draws. Building the next layer of accessible health data.
EYVA
Skin-based health scanner that reads vitals non-invasively. Consumer-first approach to deeper health data.
Longevity & family health office
Biopeak
India's first longevity clinic. 130+ biomarker panels, genomics, microbiome mapping.
Human Edge
Personalised performance and longevity programs for executives and high performers. Concierge-style preventive health.
Decode Age
Science-backed longevity supplements targeting biological aging. Evidence-led approach to extending healthspan.
Mental wellness
AI & self-help
Wysa
AI mental health chatbot used in 95 countries. $30M raised. Recommended by the NHS.
Evolve
Self-help mental wellness app with CBT-based tools. Content-led model targeting younger urban users.
Therapy & counselling
Amaha
Clinical mental health platform combining therapy, psychiatry and digital tools. ₹22.76 crore revenue in FY24
YourDOST
One of India's earliest online counselling platforms. Strong presence in universities and corporate wellness programs.
Trijog
Hybrid therapy model serving individuals, families and corporates. Online and offline care delivered through a Mumbai-based team.
Neurotech & wearables
Mave Health
Non-invasive brain stimulation device for depression and anxiety. Bringing clinical-grade neurotech to Indian consumers.
Innergize
Neurofeedback-based mental wellness wearable. Uses vagus nerve stimulation to guide stress and focus improvement.
Neuroleap
Cognitive health platform using neurotech for early detection and intervention in mental health conditions.
