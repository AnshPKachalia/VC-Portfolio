Investment thesis

As India grows wealthier and work becomes increasingly sedentary, people are beginning to invest in the one thing that no economic shift can replace — their own health. I am drawn to companies helping people move better, eat better and feel better, serving a demographic that is only going to grow. The companies I find most compelling are built by founders who have lived this themselves, with products that work well enough that customers keep coming back.
