Investment thesis

India's health and wellness market is at an inflection point — rising disposable incomes, post-pandemic awareness, and a young, aspirational consumer base are converging. The biggest opportunity is in brands that can own a behaviour, not just a product category. I focus on companies where distribution, trust, and repeat purchase are the moat.
