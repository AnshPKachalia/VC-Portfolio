Section 01

Opportunity Overview

Only What's Needed (OWN), founded by Revant Himatsingka (Food Pharmer), is a community-powered brand disrupting the Indian nutraceutical space. Launched in July 2025 following the massive Label Padhega India movement, OWN is not just a supplement brand. It is a trust infrastructure. With a $2.81M Seed round led by Mosaic Wellness, the brand is positioned to scale from a single-category protein disruptor to a multi-category Clean Food Stack.

Section 02

Market and the Problem

India's protein-based product sector was valued at approximately Rs 44,000 crore in 2025. According to IMARC Group, the industry is on a steep upward trajectory, projected to reach Rs 1,60,000 crore by 2034, representing a CAGR of approximately 15% between 2026 and 2034. However, it is plagued by structural trust issues.

  • Misleading labels. Brands often hide behind "Natural Flavors" or "Proprietary Blends" which can contain 50+ chemicals, and under-report heavy metal content.
  • Lack of testing. Most brands test only for protein content, ignoring contaminants like Melamine, Pesticides, and Aflatoxins.
  • Unnecessary additives. Brands use additives to enhance or mimic texture, flavour, and mouthfeel, often at the cost of gut health and digestion.

The clean protein segment remains nascent. Penetration is estimated at less than 10%. Prices carry a premium and taste remains a friction point for users. However, as consumer awareness rises and more brands grow in this space, OWN sits at the pinnacle of consumer trust and sets the highest bar for cleanliness and transparency in the market.

Section 03

The Founder and the Brand Moat

Revant Himatsingka has built a distribution engine with 3.3 million followers on Instagram and 1.72 million on YouTube, alongside a community that co-creates the brand by voting on flavours, packaging, and product categories.

Zero CAC Strategy

The brand's marketing is essentially a content engine that educates the consumer, leading to a near-zero customer acquisition cost that is virtually unparalleled in the industry. Most of the business comes from repeated sales and organic word of mouth.

Accountability as Competitive Edge

Revant moves beyond the celebrity-brand model by centering his identity on label scrutiny. This deep personal investment transforms transparency into a competitive edge, building an ironclad Trust Moat rooted in authentic, founder-led integrity. He has received eight legal notices from companies he has publicly called out, including two defamation suits worth Rs 2 crore each. This is perhaps the most credible signal of all: the incumbents are scared.

Section 05

The Moat: Gold Standard Transparency

OWN's competitive advantage is its reporting infrastructure. They don't just claim clean — they prove it through a 7-step validation process done on every batch produced. This is more exhaustive and transparent than any brand in India today, even those claiming to be clean.

TestPurpose and Significance
Protein ContentValidates the actual amount of protein compared to the label claim, ensuring precise delivery.
Amino Acid ProfileVerifies protein quality and exposes amino acid spiking, confirming a complete protein source without cheap additives.
Heavy Metals (Lead, Mercury)Guarantees long-term health safety by preventing intake of toxic substances, tested against strict limits.

Additionally, each packet comes with a scannable QR code linking to third-party lab tests and batch reports, with the complete amino acid profile and ingredients displayed front-of-pack in large font.

Section 06

Operational Excellence and Market Velocity

Clean Processing and Manufacturing

OWN utilises an ultra-clean manufacturing protocol, verified through a public factory tour. By partnering with a specialised manufacturer to maintain a minimalist processing flow, the company ensures the nutritional integrity of raw ingredients is preserved. This verified-clean production serves as a functional differentiator that is difficult for mass-market competitors to replicate without significant supply chain restructuring.

High Intent and Retention

While category average protein supplement retention often fluctuates due to flavour fatigue or price-switching, OWN demonstrates exceptionally high repeat purchase rates. Direct founder-to-consumer engagement allows rapid SKU iteration based on real-time feedback, turning first-time trialists into long-term subscribers. The high-intent nature of the customer base suggests a structurally lower long-term CAC, as organic word of mouth drives the majority of revenue rather than performance marketing.

Distribution: Q-Comm and D2C

OWN has successfully bypassed the Amazon/Flipkart trap by mastering high-velocity modern distribution channels. The brand has consistently emerged as a top-selling SKU in the protein category across Swiggy Instamart and Zepto, indicating strong impulse-premium demand where customers prioritise immediate availability of a trusted brand over discounted bulk options. By driving a majority of sales through its proprietary website, the brand maintains full ownership of customer data and captures higher contribution margins by avoiding heavy marketplace commissions.

Section 07

Current Issues and Risks

Margin profile note: The current gross margin profile of approximately 20% (after whey price spikes) is insufficient to support a paid growth engine. If organic word of mouth plateaus, the company lacks the margin cushion to spend on paid acquisition to reach new cohorts. The brand also structurally lacks the ability to prioritise profitability — it is a mission-first company that insists on democratic decision-making and radical transparency.
Founder concentration OWN's entire brand equity is tied to Revant's credibility. Any reputational event or sustained legal attack could significantly damage the brand. He has already received eight legal notices.
Sourcing concentration The brand is heavily exposed to global WPC pricing. Without backward integration or long-term fixed-price contracts, they are essentially price-takers in a volatile commodity market. Raw whey protein prices have nearly tripled since 2023.
Batch-test bottleneck As OWN scales from 1,000 to 100,000 tubs per month, the cost and time-lag of independent third-party testing for every batch will increase significantly. How does the brand maintain the batch-verified promise at 10x scale without delaying shipping by 14 to 21 days?
Clean label imitation Being clean is a high barrier to entry but a low barrier to imitation. Legacy giants like MuscleBlaze have deeper pockets to launch clean sub-brands if they see OWN gaining significant share. However, replicating the community governance and testing infrastructure will be genuinely difficult.
Mission vs. exit alignment A VC's exit typically requires a clear path to profitability or a high revenue multiple. A founder focused on awareness and democratic brand-building may resist the price hikes or ingredient optimisations required to make unit economics attractive for IPO or M&A.

Section 08

Future Direction: The Complete Clean Food Stack

The long-term play is to leverage this Trust Moat to move horizontally across the Indian kitchen. The vision is to build a Complete Clean Food Stack — not just supplements, but everyday food, held to the same gold standard of minimal ingredients, third-party verification, and front-of-pack transparency.

Phase 1 · Current

Premium Supplements

Whey protein, plant protein. Establishing the trust infrastructure and community co-creation model.

Phase 2 · H2 2026

Clean Snacking

Protein bars and clean-label snacks with zero seed oils. High frequency, high margin, impulse-friendly.

Phase 3 · 2027+

Daily Staples

Zero-additive spices, flours, and pantry essentials. The goal: OWN Certified carrying more weight than a standard FSSAI mark.

The OWN Certified concept is particularly interesting from a capital-light scaling perspective. If Revant's approval carries enough trust weight, it could become a licensing or curation business alongside the owned product portfolio, expanding the brand's reach without full product development risk.

Section 09

Investment Verdict

Recommendation

BUY

OWN has solved the hardest problem in D2C: trust and CAC. By building the brand in public and anchoring it to a national movement, they have created a defensible premium that legacy FMCG giants cannot easily copy without exposing their own ingredient flaws. Expansion into a full clean food stack represents a 10x growth opportunity. While the brand may almost never be highly profitable, it serves to create a movement, capture significant market share, and become the gold standard for food transparency in India.
